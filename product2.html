Overview & Details

Need a high-impact display product that gets your branding seen from distance? Our sail flags are designed for outdoor advertising, retail forecourts, exhibitions, roadside visibility, and promotional events across Dubai and the UAE.

The distinctive sail-shaped profile moves naturally in wind while keeping your branding tall, clean, and easy to spot. Whether you need a single promotional flag or a full branded setup for multiple event points, sail flags offer a practical and eye-catching solution.

Why the variant buttons matter
You can switch between fabric types directly inside the product panel and instantly see the matching size cards with pricing and usage notes. This gives you a quicker way to compare outdoor-ready options before requesting a quote.

Recommended use
Knitted Polyester is the everyday all-rounder, Mesh Polyester works better in windy outdoor spots, and Blockout Polyester is a premium option when you want denser visual coverage and stronger opacity.

Each option is built for repeat campaign use, easy assembly, and strong street-level visibility, making sail flags one of the most effective promotional tools for events and storefront marketing.

Need To Know

Frequently asked questions

Everything customers usually ask before confirming a sail flag order.

What sizes are available for sail flags?

We offer common sail flag sizes such as 2.6m, 3.5m, and 4.5m. If you need help selecting the right size, we can recommend one based on your location and viewing distance.

Can I order the flag with stand and pole set?

Yes, you can order the printed flag on its own or as a complete setup with pole set and base. We also help you choose the right base for indoor, outdoor, or event use.

How long does production usually take?

Standard production usually takes around 3 to 5 working days after artwork approval. Urgent timelines can also be discussed depending on the quantity and finishing requirements.

Can you help with artwork setup before printing?

Yes, our team can review and prepare your artwork for correct bleed, safe margins, and visual balance on the flag shape so the final print looks clean and professional.
