Buying Guide

Choose the right exhibition setup for the kind of attention you want to own

For media-heavy events, large straight or step-and-repeat backdrops keep your branding clean on camera. For booth experiences, curved walls and counters create better flow. For smaller activations, compact standees help you stay visible without taking over the floor.

  • Launches and press walls: Step-and-repeat backdrops and fabric straight displays.
  • Trade show booths: Curved pop ups, counters, and shell scheme branding.
  • Mall activations and roadshows: Portable standees and compact pop up formats.

Need To Know

Frequently asked questions

Everything customers usually ask before choosing a backdrop or exhibition display setup.

Which display type is best for a premium exhibition booth?

For a premium booth feel, fabric pop up displays and curved pop ups are usually the strongest options because they create cleaner, more continuous branding surfaces. If the setup needs to work as a media wall, step-and-repeat backdrops also perform very well.

Can I combine a backdrop with counters or smaller standees?

Yes, many event setups work best as a bundle. A main backdrop can anchor the booth, while counters support interaction points and standees help with wayfinding or message layering.

How quickly can this collection be produced?

Standard production is commonly around 4 to 6 working days after artwork approval, depending on the exact display type, quantity, and finishing requirements. Urgent event deadlines can be reviewed with our team.

Do you help with artwork sizing and template alignment?

Yes, we can review your artwork, check it against the selected display template, and guide placement, bleed, and safe areas so the final print looks balanced and professional on site.
