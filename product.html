Overview & Details

Our fabric pop up straight display is made for brands that need a clean, professional exhibition backdrop without the visual breaks of traditional panel systems. The straight wall format keeps the message clear, centered, and easy to photograph.

Use it for trade show booths, showroom launches, mall activations, conference stages, or promotional events across Dubai and the UAE. The tubular frame locks together quickly, then the printed fabric graphic pulls over the structure for a smooth, premium finish.

Why this setup works
Fabric pop up straight displays create a clean visual wall that feels more premium than rigid panel systems, while remaining fast to assemble and easy to transport between events.

Recommended use
Knitted Polyester works well for flexible event use, Taffeta Satin gives a richer premium sheen, and Super Matte is ideal where venue lights or cameras can create glare on glossy surfaces.

Every option is prepared for easy transport, quick setup, and repeat use, making it a strong choice for agencies, retail teams, and exhibition contractors.

Need To Know

Frequently asked questions

Everything customers usually ask before confirming a fabric pop up straight display order.

Which fabric material should I choose for indoor exhibitions?

Knitted Polyester is the most versatile option for regular exhibition use. Taffeta Satin works well when you want a richer finish, while Super Matte is better for camera-facing areas or venues with strong lighting.

Is the frame included with the printed backdrop?

Yes, the listed configurations include the aluminium frame, stabilizing feet, printed fabric sleeve, and carry bag unless a custom quote is requested for graphic-only supply.

How long does production usually take?

Standard production is usually around 4 to 6 working days after artwork approval. If your event deadline is close, our team can advise on the fastest feasible turnaround.

Can you help with artwork setup before printing?

Yes, we can check your design, place it on the correct template, and guide you on bleed, safe area, and logo placement so the final backdrop looks clean from edge to edge.
